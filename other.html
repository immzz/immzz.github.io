Xunmeng.org

A one-on-one fund-raising platform

The essential motivation to build this platform rooted from a volunteer activity in Guizhou province in China. We met many children who is eager to go to school but their family can't afford the cost. We collected their basic information and after coming back, we decided to build a website to help.

The platform is well designed in its donation mechanism. We offer exact family conditions but vague personal information of the children to donors. Once the donor signed an agreement to donate the child, he or she can get more detailed contact information. To ensure the donation actually happens, we have volunteers in the corresponding areas for supervision. The money will not go through us, but directly from the donor to the recipient. With these efforts, we wish to build a direct bridge between donors and recipients.
